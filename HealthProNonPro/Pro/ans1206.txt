Thank you for your question. Provided that you are regularly monitored by 
your doctor while you are on this medication, you can continue to use it as 
a sleep aid indefinitely. There are side effects of long term usage of 
Alprazoloam or any benzodiazepine class of drug which include emotional 
disturbances, nausea, headaches, dizziness, irritability, and lethargy. 
Benzodiazepines are also addictive and it is important to keep your dosage 
as low as possible to make sure you are not requiring to increase your 
dosage to gain the same effect.Thank you for consulting AskTheDoctor.com
