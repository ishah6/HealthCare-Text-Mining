Accutane or isotretinoin is commonly used to treat acne. Oral retinoids 
help control cell reproduction and have anti-inflammatory properties. They 
may even improve arthritis that accompanies psoriasis. Oral retinoids are 
also used in the treatment of severe psoriasis. Accutane is less potent 
than other retinoids such as acitretin in the treatment of psoriasis but 
nonetheless may be effective against pustular psoriasis.Taking retinoids possesses significant risks for development of birth 
defects in the fetus. If you are planning to become pregnant, you should 
not use these drugs.
