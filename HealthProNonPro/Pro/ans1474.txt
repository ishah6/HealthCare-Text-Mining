The simultaneous use of Fluoxetine (SSRI) with Triptans (for migrains), 
Demerol (painkiller) or dextromethorphan (cough medicine) may lead to an 
adverse drug reaction known as serotonin syndrome. Symptoms include 
agitation, restlessness, diarrhea, hyperthermia, nausea and vomiting, 
increased blood pressure. Also the usage of fioricet may increase the 
sedative nature of fluoxetine and elavil.
