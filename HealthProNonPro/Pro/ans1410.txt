The most probable cause of your rash may be due to tinea cruris infection. 
It is cause by a fungal infection. The infection is triggered by the 
friction and sweating in the groin area. The symptoms include itching in 
the affected area with red, scaly patches.Treatment includes : keeping the area clean and dry, wearing lose fitting 
pants and topical antifungal creams (clotimazole, miconazole, ketoconazole).
