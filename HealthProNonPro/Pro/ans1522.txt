The white fibers that is present in the mouth of your mother may be caused 
due to oral candidiasis infection. This is suggestive of a weakened 
immunity and is seen in individual with poor health, older age groups, 
weakened immunity and on steroid medication. You may need to take your 
mother to her doctor for further evaluation and tests. Oral thrush may be 
treated with proper oral hygiene and nystatin mouth washes.
