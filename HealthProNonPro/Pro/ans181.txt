Cetapin (metformin) is an oral anti-diabetic drug. It is a first line drug 
in the treatment of type 2 diabetes and is particularly helpful in 
overweight individuals. Metformin causes a few side effects : the most 
frequent being loose stools, indigestion, nausea and vomiting. These are 
minor side effects and will decrease over time.However if you notice increased tiredness, weakness, trouble breathing, 
pain abdomen and muscle pain. I would suggest you to consult your doctor 
immediately. Lactic acidosis is a serious complication of metformin.
