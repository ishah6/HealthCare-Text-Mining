The symptoms that you are experiencing could be due to tension type 
headache. This is caused when there is stress to the cervical muscles in 
the neck and scalp muscles. Usually the headache is present bilaterally and 
has a tightening or band like quality. This type of headache is extremely 
common and does not indicate anything serious. The headaches can be managed 
with rest and anti inflammatory medications like ibuprofen, naproxen etc.
