It is common to experience irregular bleeding and spotting between 
menstrual periods periods, especially during the first 3 to 6 months after 
Mirena. After your body adjusts, periods usually get lighter and the number 
of bleeding days is likely to decrease, but may remain irregular. You may 
need to consult your health-care provider if the bleeding remains heavier 
than usual or if the bleeding becomes heavy after it has been light for a 
while.
