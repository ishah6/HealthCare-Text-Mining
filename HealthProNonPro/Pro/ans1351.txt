The headaches you are suffering from is probably due to migraines.These 
headaches are often variable in intensity and duration, often unilateral 
and may be associated with nausea, vomiting and photophobia. Migraine 
headaches are a very common cause of headaches and are primarily diagnosed 
by your doctor through history and physical examination. The exact cause of 
these headaches are not known. Triggers can include stress, lack of sleep, 
certain foods like chocolate, cheese and strong lights. Treatment of 
migraines is with over the counter analgesics like NSAIDs and 
acetaminophen. More severe case will require treatment with sumatriptans to 
prevent frequent attacks. Lifestyle changes, such as avoidance of any 
identified provoking factors such as caffeine, tobacco and alcohol acn be 
helpful.
