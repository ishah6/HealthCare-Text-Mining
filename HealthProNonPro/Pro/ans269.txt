Epididymitis is an infection of the coiled tube in the back of the 
testicles. The infection is common in young males and is usually due to an 
infection from the urethra or bladder. The symptoms of Epididymitis include 
low grade fever, a feeling of pain or heaviness of the testicles, discharge 
from penis, burning sensation while passing urine and painful ejaculation. 
Treatment includes NSAIDS and antibiotics.
