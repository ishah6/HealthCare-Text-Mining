A scar tissue is formed after injury or trauma to the skin. The severity of 
the scar greatly depend on the type of injury, age and genetic factors. 
Treatment option depend on the type and severity of the scar. 
Microdermabrasion, chemical peeling may be used to hasten the recovery 
process. Laser treatment, skin surgery and grafting are the options 
available for the removal of resistant scars. A dermatologist may provide 
you with more information and treatment options
