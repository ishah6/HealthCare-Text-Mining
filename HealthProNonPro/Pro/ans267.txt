Endoscopic sinus surgery is the procedure of choice for removal of nasal 
polyps. Endoscopic sinus surgery has made the surgical treatment of polyps 
safer and improved the outcome. With appropriate preoperative evaluation 
and planning, endoscopic sinus surgery is safe.Every surgical procedure has its risks and patients undergoing preoperative 
consultation must be informed of potential orbital complications, the 
possibility of postsurgical cerebrospinal fluid leakage and possible 
bleeding from related sinus arteries.
