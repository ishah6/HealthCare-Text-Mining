Appendicitis is caused by inflammation of the appendix and is one of the 
most common causes of visit to the ER with pain abdomen. The pain that 
typically begins around the navel and then shifts to the lower right 
abdomen.Appendicitis is mainly a clinical diagnosis. Treatment usually involves 
surgery to remove the inflamed appendix. If you notice your pain increasing 
or if you have vomiting, I would suggest tha you consult your doctor for 
further investigations and management.
