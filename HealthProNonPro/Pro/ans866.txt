Methylsulfonylmethane (MSM) is an organosulfur compound used in the 
treatment of chronic pain, osteoarthritis, joint inflammation, rheumatoid 
arthritis and musculoskeletal pain. Since MSM contains sulfur, it is not 
recommend for use in persons with sulfa allergy. Other side effects of MSM 
include nausea, diarrhea, bloating, fatigue, headache, insomnia, itching, 
or worsening of allergy symptoms.
