Headaches in children are common and are usually not serious. Like adults, 
children can develop different types of headaches, including migraines or 
stress-related (tension) headaches. However it is important ot pay 
attention to headaches in children because it may indicate an infection, 
high levels of stress or anxiety, or minor head trauma. Headaches in 
children not treated with over-the-counter pain medications needs to be 
evaluated by a doctor.
