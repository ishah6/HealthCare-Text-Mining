There is no available data for Zantac(ranitidine) safety in pregnancy and I 
would adivse you to avoid taking it unless it is absolutely necessary and 
prescribed by your doctor. Lifestyle modification is the key for treating 
mild symptoms of heartburn. Smaller meals, not eating late at night, 
elevation of the head of the bed and avoiding foods and medications causing 
heartburn usually relieve the mild symptoms seen in early pregnancy. 
Chewing gum stimulates the salivary glands and can help neutralize acid. 
Abstinence from alcohol and tobacco is encouraged to reduce reflux symptoms 
and to avoid fetal exposure to these harmful substances. Antacids 
containing magnesium, aluminium or calcium can also be taken to relieve 
symptoms of heartburn.
