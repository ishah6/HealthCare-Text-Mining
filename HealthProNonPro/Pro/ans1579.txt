There is no alternative treatment for gall stone other than surgery and 
removal of the gall bladder. Delaying the surgery may make your gall 
bladder prone to infection. The stones may also obstruct the gall bladder 
duct leading to further complications. Yes, you can have a healthy life 
after gall bladder removal. The gall gladder acts as a reservoir for bile 
and its absence will not hamper any function of the body. Removal of gall 
bladder will not lead to diabetes.
