Adenomyosis refers to a disorder in which endometrial glands and stroma are 
present within the uterine musculature. Heavy menstrual bleeding and 
painful menstruation are the major symptoms of adenomyosis, occurring in 
approximately 60 and 25 percent of women, respectively. Effect of 
adenomyosis on fertility and pregnancy is still unclear. Possible 
adenomyosis cause reduced fertility. I would advise that you speak to your
