Chemical like bleach lead to a skin reaction causing burning and 
inflammation of the area. The first aid measures that your husband can take 
are :i) Put hand under running water for 10-15 minutes to flush the chemicals 
from the skin. Remove any clothing that has come into contact with the 
bleach.ii) Wrap the affected are with sterile dressing to prevent infection.iii) Topical antibiotics like silver sulfadiazine/ bacitracin / nemycin may 
be applied to prevent infections.iv) NSAIDS like advil or motrin for pain controlDo not apply any household remedy or ointment and do not disturb blisters
