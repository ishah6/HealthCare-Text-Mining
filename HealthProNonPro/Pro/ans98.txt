An increased vaginal discharge could be due to ovulation, pregnancy, 
emotional stress and infection. If pregnancy and infection is ruled out, 
the precautions that you can take are : Avoid douching, use an 
over-the-counter yeast infection treatment cream or vaginal suppository, 
use condoms, avoid using feminine hygiene sprays, fragrances, or powders 
wear cotton underwear and keep the genital area clean and dry.
