What you are experiencing is a normal vaginal discharge due to the glands 
inside the vagina and cervix. A normal vaginal discharge is usually clear 
or milky and doesn't smell bad. The discharge can be more noticeable at 
different times of the month depending on ovulation, menstrual flow, sexual 
activity and birth control.If you experience symptoms of itching, pelvic pain, rashes or fouls 
smelling discharge, you will need to see your health care provider.
