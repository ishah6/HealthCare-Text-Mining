The normal values of TSH ranges in between 0.4 to 4 mIU/L (this may vary 
with different labs). During early pregnancy, the fetus is completely 
dependent on the maternal thyroid hormone. Low concentrations of maternal 
thyroid hormones during early gestation delays neurodevelopment of the 
fetus and may lead to lower intelligence quotient scores. When a pregnant 
women is diagnosed with hypothyroidism in pregnancy, it is important to 
proactively implement a plan with her doctor to avoid exacerbation of the 
hypothyroidism.
