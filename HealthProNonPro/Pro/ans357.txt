Headaches can step from multiple causes. The headaches experienced by you

the most common type of headache and is caused due to stress. Managing a 
tension headache is often a balance between fostering healthy habits, 
finding effective non-drug treatments and using medications appropriately. 
You should try to reduce stress by incorporating some aerobic exercise into 
his routine, or meditation and yoga would also be beneficial. Pain
