A breast discharge in a women who is not pregnant may be normal but 
requires further evaluation. A normal nipple discharge may look milky, or 
it may be yellow, green. The common causes of breast discharge are breast 
abscess, medications like birth control pills, cemetidine and 
antidepressants and intra ductal papillomas. Other causes could be 
prolactinomas and breast cancer. The presence of blood in the discharge 
warrants urgent evaluation by a doctor.
