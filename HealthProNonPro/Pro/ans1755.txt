You are experiencing symptoms of menometorrhagia which presents as 
prolonged or excessive bleeding during periods. Menometrorrhagia can occur 
due to several causes. Hormonal imbalance, endometriosis, uterine fibroids 
can all cause abnormal bleeding. You may need to be evaluated by your 
doctor. Blood-work and imaging studies/ultrasound will help in determining 
the cause of the bleeding. Treatment will depend on the cause of the the 
prolonged bleed.
