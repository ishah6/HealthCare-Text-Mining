Curcumin is an a spice that has potent anti-oxidant properties. Curcumin is 
the active ingredient of turmeric and may help prevent or relieve 
inflammation, the underlying cause of the pain of arthritis. Some studies 
have found that curcumin extract was effective for treating knee arthritis 
in patients diagnosed with the condition. Curcumin is available from health 
food stores as capsules containing powder. The usual dose is 400 to 600mg 
daily, divided into three doses. Curcumin is very safe if taken within the 
recommended dosage, however it may not be recommended during pregnancy or 
during breastfeeding.
