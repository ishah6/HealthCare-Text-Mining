In the view that the regular X-rays came out negative maybe you need a 
imaging studie with more resolution to determine the nature of the lump in 
your thigh, you stated that you are overweight, possibly the lump may be a 
Lipoma , which is a benign and slow-growing tumor of fat cells, situated 
between your skin and the underlying muscle layer . It is painless and does 
not alter the results of blood work. No treatment is usually necessary for 
a lipoma. However, if the lipoma is in a location that bothers you, is 
painful or is growing, your doctor might recommend that it be removed.
