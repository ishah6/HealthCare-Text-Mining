Etoshine90 contains the active ingredient etoricoxib, which is a 
non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID). These work by blocking the 
action of cyclo-oxygenase which is responsible in producing prostaglandins.Prostaglandins cause pain, swelling and inflammation. Etoshine is used for 
pain management in diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis 
and gout. This drug has to be used in moderation in the elderly and 
patients with gastric ulcers and kidney disease. The side effects of the 
drug are bleeding due to gastric and intestinal ulceration, liver and 
kidney problems. Your doctor may need to monitor your kidney and liver 
functions when on the drug.
