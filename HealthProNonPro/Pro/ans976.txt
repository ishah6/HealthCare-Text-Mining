Panic disorder is an anxiety disorder that involves spontaneous, unexpected 
occurrences of intense fear or discomfort accompanied by physical symptoms 
such as palpitations, sweating, trembling and feeling to choking.You must see your physician for a complete physical examination and an 
in-depth history to make a definite diagnosis of an anxiety disorder. 
Treatment involves cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), psychodynamic 
psychotherapy and medication like selective serotonin-reuptake inhibitors 
(SSRIs) and benzodiazepines.There are certain lifestyle measures that you can take like :Stress managementAttend educational programs which provide information on anxiety disorders.Aerobic exerciseAvoid caffeine and alcohol
