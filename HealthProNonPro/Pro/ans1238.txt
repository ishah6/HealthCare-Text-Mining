Thank you for your question. The therapeutic efficacy of Luvox 
(Fluvoxamine) can be altered if you are using marijuana while on this 
medication. In fact, using fluvoxamine together with cannabis can increase 
fluvoxamine's side effects. These side effects include: dizziness, 
difficulty concentrating, cognitive impairment and confusion, and lastly 
drowsiness. Do not use marijuana while taking this medication.Thank you for consulting AskTheDoctor.com
