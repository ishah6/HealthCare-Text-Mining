Conservative management for a meniscal tear consists of :i) Rest, using crutches if necessary, ice pack for pain relief and 
compression with a simple elastic bandage to control the swelling. 
Elevation of the limb also helps to minimize the swelling.ii) Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs like Ibuprofen, acetaminophen and 
oxycodone will help to relieve the pain.iii) Avoiding standing for long periods of time and pivoting motions may be 
helpful while you wait for surgery.
