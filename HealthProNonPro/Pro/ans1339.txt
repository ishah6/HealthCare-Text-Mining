The difficulty with swallowing experienced by your boyfriend could be due 
to an esophageal spasm. These spasms are abnormal contraction of the 
muscles of the esophagus that cause obstruction of movement of food. The 
cause of esophageal spasms is unknown. Very hot or very cold foods may 
trigger an episode in some people. You boyfriend may need to see his doctor 
for an esophagogastroduodenoscopy or barium swallow x-ray to diagnose his 
problem. treatment will depend on the cause.
