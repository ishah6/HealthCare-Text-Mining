Both Diclofenac and Flurbiprofen are (NSAIDS) non-steroidal 
anti-inflammatory drugs taken to reduce pain (caused by migraine in your 
case). Though these drugs have other side effects like causing 
gastrointestinal ulceration, bleeding and liver damage, long term use does 
not lead to addiction.You have also mentioned that you are presently taking alprazolam, a 
benzodiazepine. Long term use of this drug may cause drug dependence and 
withdrawal symptoms on discontinuing the drug. You may want to consult with 
your doctor for further information regarding the management of your 
migraines and the side effect of these medication.
