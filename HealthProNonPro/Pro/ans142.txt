Blepharitis, which is an inflammation at the base of your eyelashes that 
produces the thick, yellowish discharge causing sticky eyes in the morning. 
Treatment includesi) Application of heat to warm the eyelid gland secretions and to promote 
evacuation and cleansing of the secretory passages.ii) Washing the eyelid to mechanically to remove adherent material.iii) Antibiotic ointment (erythromycin) is applied to the eyelid margin.Resistant cases may need may need topical corticosteroids, combined with an 
antibiotic and oral antibiotics.
