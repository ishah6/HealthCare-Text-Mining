Clear cell acanthoma is a benign, slow growing epithelial skin tumour. It 
is usually a solitary lesion and affects the middle aged and elderly. The 
lesion is dome-shaped or nodular and can vary from 3-20 mm in diameter.Clear cell acanthomas can persist for many years without causing any 
complication or changing into a malignant tumour. The treatment is by 
surgical excision.
