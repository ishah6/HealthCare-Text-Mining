characteristic circular shaped rash that can increase in size, become 
inflamed and very itchy is evident. Treatment of tinea corporis normally responds to over the counter 
antifungal medication containing Terbinafine, which you can use as 
directed. If this infection does not clear up in two weeks, please follow
