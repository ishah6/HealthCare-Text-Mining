Patients with COPD are susceptible to infections which may rapidly 
deteriorate their condition. It is estimated that 70 to 80 percent of COPD 
exacerbations are due to respiratory infections. Viral and bacterial 
infections cause most exacerbation, whereas atypical bacteria are a 
relatively uncommon cause. The remaining 20 to 30 percent are due to 
environmental pollution or have an unknown etiology. Successful management
