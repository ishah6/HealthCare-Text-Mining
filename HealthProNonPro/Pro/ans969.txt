Pain the the foot may be caused due to tendinitis, a calcaneal spur, 
plantar fasciitis and inflammatory disorders such as arthritis. Usually 
foot pain is treated with rest, ice, compression bandage and painkillers. 
If these do not improve your symptoms then you may have to consult your 
doctor for further investigations like a complete blood count, uric acid 
levels and x-ray of the foot.
