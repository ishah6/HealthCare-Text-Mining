Hodgkin's lymphoma is a lymphoid malignancy involving the lymph nodes, 
spleen, bone marrow. Hodgkin's lymphoma often presents as a painless 
swollen lymph node. The other constitutional symptoms include unexplained 
weight loss, fever, night sweats. These symptoms are present in 40% of 
cases.Though your swollen lymph node may be due Hodgkin's lymphoma, other 
conditions like infectious mononucleosis, tuberculosis, SLE and 
toxoplasmosis needs to be rules out. The diagnosis of hodgkin;s lymphoma 
will depend on blood studies like complete blood count, ESR, bone marrow 
biopsies and CT scan of chest, abdomen and pelvis.
