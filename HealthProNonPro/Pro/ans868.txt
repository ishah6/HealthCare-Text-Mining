Metronide (metronidazole) is an antibiotic that is usually prescribed for 
anaerobic bacterial infection of the gut. Taking metronide will not provide 
you immediate relief. There are some measures that you can take at home to 
relieve the pain.i) Rinse your mouth with warm salt water.ii) Use dental floss to dislodge and food material that is caught in 
between the teeth.iii) Over the counter pain medicines to relieve the pain.iv) You can use local topical anesthetic agents containing benzocaine to 
provide temporary relief. Application of clove oil may also help.If the symptoms are not relieved with these measures, you will need to see 
your dentist right away.
