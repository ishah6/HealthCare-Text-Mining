Hyperthyroidism is a condition caused by excessive thyroid hormones 
produced by the thyroid gland. The common cause of hyperthyroidism are 
thyroiditis, graves disease and toxic multi-nodular goiter. Hyperthyroidism 
is more common in females than in men. Though hyperthyroidism is usually 
seen in third to fourth decade of life, it can be also present much earlier.Diagnosis of hyperthyroidism involves testing for thyroid function (T3, T4 
and TSH). The prognosis of hyperthyroidism is good with therapy.
