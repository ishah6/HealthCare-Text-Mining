The swelling that your daughter has developed on either side of the neck is 
probably due to enlarged lymph nodes caused by inflammation of the 
surrounding tissues. It is unlikely that your daughter has mumps.Mumps is a viral infection that primarily afftects the parotid glands 
present just below the ears. Symptoms include swollen salivary glands, pain 
during chewing or swallowing, fever, weakness and fatigue. Medication like 
antihistamines and tylenol will help reduce the pain and swelling.
