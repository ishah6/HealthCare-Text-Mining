It is possible that this swelling the area of your cut could be a keloid. A 
keloid is formed in in the area of a scar when excessive collagen is 
deposited in this area. As a result a hard, swelling can form and depending 
on its position, it can become painful. It is also possible that during 
your injury, you may have sustained minor damage to the nerve, which could 
in fact lead to further pain. We recommend that you have your doctor 
examine this to determine if you have a keloid and sustained any nerve
