Femulen is a contraceptive pill containing only progestogen, also called 
progestogen-only pill or mini pill. This pill is safer for women who are 
older than 35. It is also safer for women who smoke, have high blood 
pressure, are overweight or have a history of blood clots.Yasmin is a combined oral contraceptive pill containing ethinylestradiol 
and drospirenone. If you have a high blood pressure, smoke, increased 
weight, headaches and risk for stroke, I would suggest that you consult 
your doctor for contraceptive pill containing only progestogen.
